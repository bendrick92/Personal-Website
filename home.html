About Me

Welcome to my website! My name is Ben Walters, and I'm a web developer from Rochester, MN.

I am passionate about finding elegant solutions to complex problems and creating user-friendly experiences across the web. I enjoy experimenting with new technologies and strive to continuously learn from those around me.

In my free time I enjoy reading, programming, spending time with my girlfriend, and riding my bike!
