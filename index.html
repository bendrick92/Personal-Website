About Me

Welcome! My name is Ben Walters, and I'm a web developer from Rochester, MN.

I have several years development experience in the educational, publishing, and medical certification industries.

I am passionate about finding elegant solutions to complex problems and creating user-friendly experiences across the web. I enjoy experimenting with new technologies and strive to continuously learn from those around me.

In my free time I enjoy reading, programming, spending time with friends, riding my bike, and working on my car.

I'm also an active member of many Minnesota car clubs, and have a separate blog dedicated to cars.

If you have any questions about my projects or are interested in working together, feel free to shoot me an email at contact@bpwalters.com!
